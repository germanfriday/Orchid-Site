Guinot Paris at Orchid Day Spa

Founded in France over forty years ago by Rene Guinot, a well-known chemist, this collection of skincare products, now known throughout the world as Guinot Paris, began with a focused approach to treating the skin with the now well-known Hydradermie treatment. Today, Guinot Paris skincare as well as the Guinot professional treatments are available in a specially selected number of spas and salons throughout the U.S. and the world.

Guinot Paris provides a wide range of beauty care, covering three specific categories - face, body and sun. In each category, the company's philosophy of professional expertise as a guide to product usage is an important part of its skincare message. Basic to this is Guinot's understanding that every skin is different and is being exposed to different environmental and stressful conditions at different times. It is the synergy of the professional services and the retail products that together brings about skin beauty on which Guinot Paris prides itself.

Hydradermie

Hydradermie will restore skins natural beauty with a customized treatment to meet your skin's individual needs. The secrets behind the treatment include ionization, oxygenation, and a lifting massage. The exceptional treatment for radiant skn.

Aromatics

You will find tranquil beauty with this unique method that combines the benefits of the essential oils with de-stressing and tension reducing massage techniques. Beaute Neuve

Beaute Neuve will restore the radiance in your skin that is often lost from stress, exhaustion, and from the passing of time. The secrets of the treatment include gentle exfoliation and in depth diffusion. The peeling effect will restore radiance and allow the skin to breathe, as gentle fruit acids recreate a new skin.

Liftosome

With age or fatigue we notice a decrease in the skin's elasticity. Liftosome will lift and firm with extracts of orange, ginseng, marine collagen, and vitamin C, while filling the skin with new energy, restore its elasticity, and regenerate new cells. The secrets behind the treatment include vitamin enriched serum, the thermal treatment mask, and the massage with chitin.
