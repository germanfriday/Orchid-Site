Payment Options

We accept MasterCard, Visa, American Express, Discover, SpaFinder Certificates, cash and personal checks as payment for services rendered at Orchid Day Spa.

Gift Certificates

Gift certificates are available at the front desk. They are available in dollar amounts, select services, or for our spa packages and can be used up to one year from date of purchase. We accept phone orders and mail orders, where the gift certificate(s) may be picked up or mailed directly to the recipient.

Consultations

All spa consultations are complimentary.

Return Policy

No refunds or exchanges on products or services. Gift certificates are non-refundable or redeemable for cash.

Cancellations/Changes

Please allow at least 24 hours notice to cancel or change any scheduled service. Please be advised that we may charge a fee for two or more services scheduled.

Spa Parties

Please call our reception desk for details.

Please Note:

Gratuity is not included in spa services or spa packages

but always appreciated..
